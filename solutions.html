Whistler Intel

Maximize your ski and ride time. Every run, every day.

The Problem

Lost Time in Lift Lines

Large resorts mean long, unpredictable lift lines that eat into ride time.

No reliable real-time data on which lifts are congested vs. which are clear.

Skiers and riders waste valuable mountain time guessing where to go next.

The Solution

AI-Powered Real-Time Routing

  • ✓ Routes you to the least busy, nearest lift in real time
  • ✓ AI crowd counting verifies lift line congestion using visual data
  • ✓ Crowdsourced confirmations from other users on the mountain
  • ✓ Factors in user skill level and terrain preferences for personalized recommendations

How It Works

Multi-Source Intelligence

AI Vision

Computer vision counts crowds at lift bases and across slopes for accurate real-time data.

Crowd Sourcing

Users confirm and report live conditions, creating a community-verified data layer.

Smart Routing

Algorithm combines proximity, wait time, skill level, and terrain type to recommend your next lift.

Personalization

Set your skill level and terrain preferences to get routes matched to how you ride.
